They possess a strong understanding of programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and often work with various frameworks and tools to create dynamic, user-friendly online experiences. Web developers collaborate with designers,
Content creators, and other stakeholdersx to ensure that websites are not only visually appealing but also functional and optimized for performance.
